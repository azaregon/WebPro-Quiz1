My Hometown

Surabaya

Surabaya, also known with it's nickname "City of Heroes," is the capital of Jawa Timur province, Indonesia. Surabaya is the second-largest city in Indonesia with beautiful scenery and has a lot of history behind it. One history that makes Surabaya called "City of Heroes" is the 10th November movement. At that time, the people of Surabaya were attacked by the Britain army because their general, A.W.S Mallaby, died because of a grenade explosion near Jembatan Merah. To remind the next generation about that heroic movement of Surabaya, now, November 10 is celebrated as the Indonesia National Heroes day. Not just that, there is also a university in Indonesia with the name taken from that, it's called "Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember" or ITS.

Besides history, Surabaya has a lot of things to do, from culinary, and also spots. In culinary, we have Semanggi as our special food. It is a clover based food with peanut sauce and Puli Crackers. see food of surabaya

If you have a lot of time to enjoy Surabaya, I suggest you to come and see one of the iconic places, Jalan Tunjungan. it is a street with colonial vibes. This street is also has a historic story behind it, you should check it out. If you need more destination, see surabaya places
